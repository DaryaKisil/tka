The project itself is devoted to the products of TKA company, which focuses on producing different means of measuring instruments.
Software was prodused for a special model of lux meter, which is used for measuring brightness in lux, fc or cd/m². 
The measurement of light intensity with a lux meter is becoming increasingly important in the workplace due to safety concerns.

******text from the video*******

Good afternoon. Now we are going to look at a small software for TKA, a company that produces optical measuring instruments. 
In particular, the software is written for the TKA-PKM(05) lux meter.
To begin, in order to connect our instrument, press the Update button so that it is displayed among those available for connection.
Then press the Connect button to connect to it and the graph starts to show data from the instrument's sensor. 
To disconnect the instrument and connect a new one, press the Disconnect button and select another available comport. 
There is also the Pause button, which stops the display. 
Additionally, there is a zoom change in case of an abrupt change in the sensor data.
